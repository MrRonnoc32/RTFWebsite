Bridge Builder: Leading with Purpose and Courage to Create Opportunity for All

A leadership memoir and playbook for anyone tasked with building bridges in a divided world.

Part story, part playbook, Bridge Builder equips leaders at every level to transform how they show up — for themselves, their teams, and their communities. Rooted in her journey through education and civic leadership, Rachael shares her Heal. Think. Lead. framework, offering practical strategies, reflective prompts, and real‑world lessons that apply across sectors.
