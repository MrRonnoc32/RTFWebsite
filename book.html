Meaningful Change Begins Within

We live in a time of division, uncertainty, and rapid change.

A Bridge Within is a deeply personal leadership story and practical guide for anyone seeking to create meaningful change in a complex world.

Drawing on her experiences as a teen mother, educator, nonprofit leader, and civic bridge-builder, Rachael Tutwiler Fortune explores a simple but powerful idea:

Before we can transform our organizations, communities, and relationships, we must first do the work within ourselves.

"
Rachael Tutwiler Fortune offers a powerful and timely vision for leadership, one that recognizes that the capacity to create opportunity in the world begins with the development of the human being. This relational intelligence is a deeply needed perspective for leaders navigating complexity and change.

Isabelle C. Hau Executive Director, Stanford Accelerator for Learning Author, Love to Learn
